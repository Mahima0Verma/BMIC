About Us

“ Education is the key to success in life, and teachers make a lasting impact in the lives of their students. ”

The Babu Mahadev Inter College has been founded by the Mr. Bhai Lal Kol (M.L.A of Chhanbey vidhansabha Mirzapur). The purpose of school is to impart the value education among the rural area children. The main motive of the school is to develop the overall personality of a child through many extra curricular activities. We focus on the overall development of a child instead of giving only bookish knowledge.

Medium of Instruction

The medium of instruction is Hindi

Academic Session

The Academic Session starts from April to March for classes Nursery to XII

Location of the School

The school is situated in Jhelampura Barkachh Dubar Kalan Lalganj Mirzapur .

COURSES OF STUDY

Our school is affiliated to UP Board , Prayagraj. We are following the N.C.E.R.T syllabus from class I to XII.

PARENTS TEACHER MEET

Parents - Teacher's meeting is held after each examination.
